Software Defined Radio Receiver Head

Responsible for filtering out signals outside of the bandwidth. Uses a quadrature mixer to down convert incoming signals to intermediate frequency (lower frequency) which is then used by the demodulater to decode the signal. Produces inphase/quadrature signals with phase accuracy of 86% and higher. (Credit to my partners Sadra Shirdarreh and Junyi Liu).

  • Altium Designer
  • Mulitsim
  • PyVisa
